
# Troubleshooting Guide for the M‑CHAIN Dataset Manifest

This troubleshooting guide helps resolve common issues users may encounter when downloading, organizing, or verifying the datasets used in the M‑CHAIN evaluation.

The datasets referenced here remain at their **official sources**:
- SmartBugs Curated (labeled dataset) [1](https://github.com/smartbugs/smartbugs-curated)  
- SmartBugs Wild (~47k real‑world contracts) [2](https://github.com/smartbugs/smartbugs-wild)  
- SWC Registry test cases (canonical samples, not actively maintained since 2020) [3](https://github.com/SmartContractSecurity/SWC-registry)[4](https://swcregistry.io/)  

For dataset structure, sizes, and usage notes, refer to SmartBugs dataset documentation.  
[5](https://github.com/smartbugs/smartbugs/blob/master/doc/datasets.md)
